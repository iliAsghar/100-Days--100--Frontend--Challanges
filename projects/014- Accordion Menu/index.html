Interesting Facts!

Koalas

Koalas are marsupials from Australia that spend most of their time in eucalyptus trees, eating leaves and sleeping up to 22 hours a day.

Planet Mars

Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun, known for its red color due to iron oxide on its surface. It has the largest volcano and canyon in the solar system!

The Letter i

The letter "I" is the ninth letter of the English alphabet and is unique for its simplicity, consisting of a single vertical line. In Roman numerals, "I" represents the number one.
